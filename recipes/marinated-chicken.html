Perfect Marinated Chicken Breasts

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

ingredients

  • ½ cups lemon-lime soda
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

How to Make Marinated Chicken Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade marinated chicken:

Step 1

Whisk lemon-lime soda, olive oil, soy sauce, and garlic powder in a bowl and pour into a resealable plastic bag. Add chicken breasts, coat with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator overnight.

Step 2

Preheat a grill to medium heat and lightly oil the grate.

Step 3

Remove chicken from marinade and discard used marinade. Cook chicken breasts on preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 7 to 8 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
